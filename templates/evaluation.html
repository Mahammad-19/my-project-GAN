Metric Guide
SSIM — Structural Similarity
Measures luminance, contrast, and structural similarity between real and synthetic images. Range 0–1. Values above 0.7 are considered good.
PSNR — Peak Signal-to-Noise Ratio
Measures signal reconstruction quality in decibels. Higher values indicate less noise and better image fidelity. Typical good values: 25–40 dB.
FID — Fréchet Inception Distance
Measures distributional distance between real and generated images. Lower means synthetic images are statistically closer to real ones.
